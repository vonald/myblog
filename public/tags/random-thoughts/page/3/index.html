Blogging for me is a way to process thoughts. This is mostly an in-out system. Stuff happens, I think about it, I write. Not everything ends up on the blog. Many sentences jotted down go no further. But things that need working out in more depth will probably end up as a post. For the last week though, I’ve been in a log-jam. Too much to process.
