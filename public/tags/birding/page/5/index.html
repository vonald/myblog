March 15, 2020

Our Victoria plum tree is covered in buds so am hoping for a good crop this year...

Our Victoria plum tree is covered in buds so am hoping for a good crop this year. Yesterday I spotted a pigeon happily devouring them so shooed it away. Last year we didn’t get a single fruit for the same reason. I’m glad I saw the pigeon as it prompted me to get some netting over said tree in order to prevent further loss of blossom. The birds are getting very busy...

The birds are getting very busy in our garden. All three nest boxes have had attention of some sort. A pair of robins are nesting in one while a pair of blue tits seem very interested in another, spending a lot of time checking it out.
