June 21, 2020

None

Went for a short family walk on the Wiltshire Downs that turned into a respectable 6k. Wildflowers and birds and a big sky. Skylark, whitethroat, blackcap, yellow hammer, corn bunting and willow warbler. Took this photo from a long barrow. And according to the map, crossed a Roman road. History beneath our feet.
#birding | #nature | #walking

May 17, 2020

None

A family walk this morning. Took a trip out to the Somerset Levels, now that we are allowed to travel a bit more. Lots of birds - but no cuckoos. Swifts, sedge warblers, curlews, reed buntings, skylarks, egrets and swans. Meadows looking lovely. Lots of flowers including orchids (Southern Marsh?). Drama in the garden when we returned.
